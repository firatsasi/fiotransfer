What Awaits You

Tour Highlights

A curated journey through the most breathtaking natural and cultural landmarks within reach of Istanbul.

Sapanca Lake

Crystal clear freshwater lake nestled between forested mountains. Scenic lakeside walks, tranquil views, and the purest air in the region.

Maşukiye Village

Enchanting mountain village famous for its cascading waterfalls, lush forest trails, and farm-to-table organic lunch experiences.

Kartepe Mountain

Sweeping panoramic views over Sapanca Lake and the Marmara plain. Optional cable car ride to the summit for unforgettable scenery.

Optional Activities

Elevate your adventure with ATV safari through mountain trails, zipline over the forest canopy, or quad biking — available on request.
