What Awaits You

Tour Highlights

Istanbul's greatest landmarks, ancient markets, and waterways — curated into one extraordinary private day.

Hagia Sophia & Blue Mosque

Stand inside one of humanity's greatest buildings — the 1,500-year-old Hagia Sophia — then cross to the iconic six-minareted Blue Mosque.

Topkapi Palace

600 years of Ottoman imperial history. Explore the legendary treasury, sacred relics, and the harem of the sultans with your private guide.

Grand Bazaar

Lose yourself in 4,000 shops across 61 covered streets. One of the world's oldest and largest covered markets, alive with colour and culture.

Bosphorus Experience

The mythical strait dividing two continents. Dine at a waterfront restaurant or join a private Bosphorus cruise for unforgettable views.

Istanbul Private City Tour — Discover the City in Luxury

An Istanbul private city tour with Fio Transfer is the most refined way to experience one of the world's greatest cities. Unlike crowded group tours with fixed schedules, our fully private Istanbul tour is tailored entirely to your interests and pace. Your expert English-speaking chauffeur will guide you to the landmarks that define Istanbul: the awe-inspiring Hagia Sophia, the opulent Topkapi Palace, the vibrant Grand Bazaar, the iconic Blue Mosque, and the shimmering Bosphorus waterfront. Every stop is chosen to give you the perfect balance of history, culture, and atmosphere.

Travelling in your dedicated Mercedes-Maybach, you will enjoy seamless transfers between each site with no waiting for buses or sharing your experience with strangers. Whether you want to photograph the Golden Horn at sunrise, bargain for spices in the Egyptian Bazaar, or simply cruise along the Bosphorus Strait with the European and Asian shorelines stretching before you, your Istanbul private tour is completely yours to shape. Fio Transfer's private Istanbul city tour is available daily and can be booked instantly via WhatsApp — making it the easiest, most luxurious way to explore this extraordinary city.
